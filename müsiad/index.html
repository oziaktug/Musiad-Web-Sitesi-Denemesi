Musiad Visionary

MUSIAD VISIONARY Summit is an event held for the benefit of society in order to inform business people in Turkey, prepare them for the future and help them develop trust-oriented business connections.The first was under the title of “Doing Business with the Future” in 2015, the second was “New Turkey in a Transforming World” in 2017, the third was “Digital Future” in 2019, and the fourth was “Make a Difference in Digital, Climate, Initiative, Transformation and Values!” The summit, which was held with the theme of the summit, is brought to life with the participation of leading business people, academics and senior government executives from Turkey and the world.

International Business Forum

IBF (International Business Forum) is an international platform established by MUSIAD in 1995, with the participation of businessmen from the business world of 25 countries, and the annual congress and accompanying commercial activities. Established on the principle of establishing a “Global Business Network” among Muslim Businessmen, this elite platform is managed by an executive board (BOG) consisting of 42 businessmen’s associations from 25 countries and aims to establish economic ties between countries, to create sustainable cooperation models, to strengthen communication and to support social and economic relations. has adopted the principle of accelerating development.
